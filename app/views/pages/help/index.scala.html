What is Peripleo?

Peripleo is a map-based search engine to data published by partners of Pelagios Commons, a Digital Humanities initiative aiming to foster better linkages between online resources documenting the past.

Peripleo is not a reposity - all data you can find here remains hosted at our partners own sites. It merely provides a unified index to search and explore across all these datasets.

Data in Peripleo is pretty diverse. Right now, most of our partners publish information about ancient places and phyiscal objects connected to them, such as archaeological finds. But Pelagios Commons is growing, and we are beginning to include data about people, time periods, and even fulltexts of geo-tagged literature, and transcribed scans of historic maps.
