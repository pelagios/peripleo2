Peripleo [periËˆpleo] — ancient Greek for “to sail (or swim) around” in the sense of exploration or discovery — is a search engine to Pelagios Commons, a Digital Humanities initative aiming to build better connections between scholars, projects and institutions studying places of the past.

Peripleo provides an interface to explore data about past places, people, objects, time periods, and navigate the connections between them.
